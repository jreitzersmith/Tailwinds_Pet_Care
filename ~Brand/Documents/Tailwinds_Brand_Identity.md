# Tailwinds Pet Care, LLC — Brand Identity Guide

**Last Updated:** June 17, 2026

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Primary / Headers | Lucida Bright | Serif; used for company name and headings |
| Body / General Text | Lucida Sans Unicode | Clean sans-serif; used for body copy and details |
| Accent / Decorative | Lucida Calligraphy | Script; used sparingly for taglines or highlights |
| Fallback | Arial | System fallback if Lucida fonts unavailable |

> All font choices are consistent with the company letterhead.

## Logo

| File | Format | Usage |
|------|--------|-------|
| `Tailwinds_Logo.png` | PNG (raster) | Web, documents, general use |
| `Tailwinds_Logo.svg` | SVG (vector) | Print, large-format, scalable use |

**Design notes:**
- Logo incorporates pet silhouette with travel/aviation vehicle motifs (plane, SUV, RV)
- Concept reflects the airline-employee customer base (Southwest & American Airlines)
- Do not stretch or distort the logo; scale proportionally only
- Preferred background: white or light blue (`#A0AFC5`)

---

## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Warm, trustworthy, professional with a travel-friendly spirit
- **Target audience:** Airline employees (Southwest Airlines, American Airlines) in the DFW area
- **Key message:** Your pets are in good hands while you're in the air

## Pets Served

Dogs · Cats · Birds · Reptiles · Fish · Small Rodents & Mammals

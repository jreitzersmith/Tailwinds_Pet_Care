## 2. Name Availability Research

**Method:** Queried the Texas Comptroller's public Franchise Tax (taxable-entity) database, which covers LLCs/LPs/corporations statewide.

**Findings:**
- Statewide there are ~200 entities with "Tailwind"/"Tailwinds" in the name; **80 of those have DFW-area mailing addresses** (largest cluster is the Tailwind Advisors family-office network in Fort Worth, 76107).
- **"Tailwinds Pet Care" returned ZERO matches** — along with "Tailwind Pet Care," "Tailwinds Pet," "Tailwind Pet," and "Tailwinds Petcare." The name appears clear in the Comptroller's records.
- Nearest animal-themed names (all distinguishable from "Pet Care"): **Tailwinds & Tails LLC** (Farmers Branch), **Tailwinds and Wags LLC** (Austin), plus bare **Tailwinds, Inc.** (Huntsville) and **Tailwinds, L.L.C.** (Houston).
- No online presence exists for the two pet-themed LLCs, and "Tailwinds Pet Care" does not appear anywhere online.

**Distinguishability:** Texas uses a "distinguishable in the records" test. Adding the descriptive words "Pet Care" creates wording differences beyond a mere designator/punctuation, so "Tailwinds Pet Care, LLC" should read as distinguishable from the bare "Tailwinds" entities.

**Caveats (important):**
- The Comptroller database is **not** the binding determination. The Secretary of State (SOS) makes the final availability call at filing, and SOS records also include **active name reservations** (120-day holds) and **assumed-name/DBA filings** that the Comptroller search does not show.
- Authoritative pre-filing check = **SOSDirect** (~$1 per search).
- Entity-name availability is **not** trademark clearance. There are pet businesses named "Tailwinds" in other states; a separate USPTO/Texas trademark search is advisable if branding matters.

## 4. Texas SOS Filing Process & Timing

**Document:** Certificate of Formation (Form 205). **Fee: $300** (~$308 online due to ~2.7% card surcharge; mail requires a check/money order for exactly $300).

**Processing times (fluctuate with SOS queue volume):**
- **Online via SOSDirect** — fastest; ~2 business days recently, up to ~10–12 business days when busy. **Effective/formation date = the day you file**, regardless of when review completes. No need to pay for expedite — the online queue is already the fast lane.
- **SOSUpload** — ~13–15 business days.
- **Mail** — 4–8 weeks plus mail transit (2–3 months total).

**Expedited options (Texas Express program, effective Oct 1, 2025):**
- Standard Expedited — $50 per document (~2–3 business days).
- Next-Day — $500 per document (received by 12:00 p.m., processed by close of next business day).
- Same-Day — $750 per document (received by 12:00 p.m., processed same day).
- Next-day and same-day **must be submitted in person or by courier** at the SOS office, 400 W. 15th Street, Austin.

**To avoid rejection delays:** confirm name clearance and complete, consistent registered-agent info before submitting.

## 5. Assumed Name (DBA) Considerations

- Since **September 2019 (Texas HB 3609)**, registered entities like LLCs file assumed-name certificates with the **SOS only — not the county**.
- County-clerk DBA records now cover only **unincorporated** businesses (sole proprietorships / general partnerships).
- Practical impact: any future DBA for the LLC goes to the SOS. A Dallas County search only catches someone running "Tailwinds Pet Care" informally without an entity.
- Dallas County records portal: **dallas.tx.publicsearch.us** → record type "Assumed Name" → search "Tailwinds" (free to search; could not be auto-queried because the portal's search API is gated).

## 6. IRS / EIN

- A federal Employer Identification Number (EIN) is **free** directly from the IRS at irs.gov.
- Do **not** pay any third-party site for an EIN — they resell a free government service.

## 7. Insurance & Bonding (Pet-Care Specific)

Not legally required for pet care in Texas, but standard and client-expected.

**General liability insurance:**
- Budget route — membership-based pet-care insurers (e.g., Pet Sitters Associates, Pet Care Insurance): starts in the low $100s/year for $1M coverage.
- Standalone commercial GL: ~$40–45/month (~$480–540/year average).
- Broader policies bundling property/auto: ~$53–131/month.

**Fidelity / surety bond:**
- ~$100–$400/year for a standard $10,000 bond. Not required, but widely used; reassures clients.

**CRITICAL coverage gap to watch:** Generic GL / BOP policies typically cover injury to third parties and their property, but usually **exclude injury, illness, or death of the animals in your care**. That risk requires a specific **"animal bailee" / "care, custody & control" (CCC) endorsement**. Pet-specific insurers include this in their base policy — it's the main reason most pet pros use them over a generic carrier. Always confirm CCC/animal-bailee coverage is included before buying any policy.

---

## 8. USAA Insurance — Assessment

- USAA has sold small-business insurance since 2021 (general liability, BOP, commercial excess underwritten in-house; most other lines brokered to third-party partners via USAA Insurance Agency). Pricing roughly $22–$129+/month.
- **Membership required** (military/veteran/family eligibility).
- Pet-care eligibility is **not guaranteed** — USAA publishes no list of covered industries; a quote is needed.
- If quoting USAA, explicitly confirm the policy includes **animal bailee / care, custody & control** coverage — without it the biggest risk is uncovered.
- USAA does **not** issue surety/fidelity bonds — source the bond separately regardless.

## 9. Cost Summary

**One-time formation:**
- SOS Certificate of Formation: ~$300–308
- IRS EIN: $0
- Subtotal: **~$300–308 one-time**

**Recurring annual (insurance + bonding):**
- Lean first year: ~$308 formation + ~$150 GL (association) + ~$100 bond ≈ **$560**
- Fuller coverage: ~$308 + ~$540 standalone GL + ~$300 bond ≈ **$1,150**

**Other notes:**
- Texas has **no annual LLC fee**. An annual franchise tax report is required but **$0 is owed** below the ~$2.47M revenue no-tax-due threshold (informational filing only).
- Registered agent: **$0 if you serve as your own**; ~$100–150/year for a commercial agent.

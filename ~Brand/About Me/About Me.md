I am a long time pet owner.

I've personally had dogs, cats, rats, gerbils, guinea pigs, snakes, lizards, turtles, and fish. I currently have two dogs, an American Eskimo and a red Boston Terrier

In a past life, I worked at Petsmart for 6 years. Primarily as the Department Manager for the Specialty Pet Care department. I was responsible for managing the care, cleaning, and attention of all of the non-canine/feline creatures. Including fish, turtles, snakes, lizards, guinea pigs, hamsters, rats, gerbils, mice, chinchillas, parakeets, finches, lovebirds, conures, and parrots.

While at Petsmart, I absolutely loved being able to see all the guest pets that would accompany their pet parents on shopping trips. One of my favorite animals during my time at Petsmart was an African Grey parrot. It was with us for 13 months before finding a forever home. During that time, we became attached...to the point where his favorite phrase that he would repeatedly call out was "John, to the Aviary" which is what the cashiers would announce over the intercom when customers wanted to see the birds and small mammals. Grey knew that calling that out would bring me to his aviary so, on his own, he started saying it when he wanted a treat, or simple attention. Some of my most treasured moments at Petsmart were the conures and Emerald Green lizards that I raised from egg till adoption.
